Key Features

Everything needed for a complete bias audit

Smart Upload
Drag-and-drop CSV with auto-detection of protected attributes (gender, race, age) and outcome columns.
Visual Dashboard
Interactive bar charts, radar graphs, and per-group approval rate tables. Fairness grade A–F with animated score rings.
Gemini Explainer
Click any finding → Gemini 2.5 flash explains what it means, who is harmed, legal implications, and exact remediation steps.
What-If Simulator
Toggle attributes off and watch fairness score animate in real time. Find the minimum change needed to reach a fair model.
PDF Audit Report
One click → Gemini writes a 3-page professional audit with findings, legal risk, recommendations. Downloadable instantly.
Analysis History
All past analyses saved in Firestore. Review, compare, and track fairness improvements over time.
